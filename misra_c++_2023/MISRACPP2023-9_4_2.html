MISRACPP2023-9_4_2 
Rule 9.4.2 (Required) The structure of a switch statement shall be appropriate
RULES

The first or the last label of a switch statement should be the 'default' label unless all enumeration values are tested [MISRACPP2023-9_4_2-a-2]
Every switch branch should be unconditionally terminated [MISRACPP2023-9_4_2-b-2]
A switch label shall only be used when the most closely-enclosing compound statement is the body of a switch statement [MISRACPP2023-9_4_2-c-2]
A switch statement shall only contain switch labels and switch clauses, and no other code [MISRACPP2023-9_4_2-d-2]
Every switch statement shall have at least two switch-clauses [MISRACPP2023-9_4_2-e-2]
The 'switch' statement should not contain labels other than 'case' and 'default' [MISRACPP2023-9_4_2-f-2]
The condition of a 'switch' statement should only be preceded by an optional simple-declaration [MISRACPP2023-9_4_2-g-2]
