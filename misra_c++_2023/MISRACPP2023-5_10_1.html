MISRACPP2023-5_10_1 
Rule 5.10.1 (Required) User-defined identifiers shall have an appropriate form
RULES

Do not declare entities in the standard library namespaces: posix, std and stdN [MISRACPP2023-5_10_1-a-2]
Do not use identifiers which begin with one or two underscores (`_' or `__') [MISRACPP2023-5_10_1-b-2]
An identifier shall not contain a double underscore '__' [MISRACPP2023-5_10_1-c-2]
User defined suffixes of the user defined literal operators shall start with underscore followed by one or more letters [MISRACPP2023-5_10_1-d-2]
A user-defined literal suffix should be directly preceded by a literal operator [MISRACPP2023-5_10_1-e-2]
A macro identifier should have an appropriate form [MISRACPP2023-5_10_1-f-2]
Do not declare identifiers with names reserved in the standard library [MISRACPP2023-5_10_1-g-2]
