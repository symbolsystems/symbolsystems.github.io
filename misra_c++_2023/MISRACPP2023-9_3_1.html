MISRACPP2023-9_3_1 
Rule 9.3.1 (Required) The body of an iteration-statement or a selection-statement shall be a compound-statement
RULES

The statement forming the body of a 'switch', 'while', 'do...while' or 'for' statement shall be a compound statement [MISRACPP2023-9_3_1-a-2]
'if' and 'else' should be followed by a compound statement [MISRACPP2023-9_3_1-b-2]
