CERT-EXP44_C (RULE) Do not rely on side effects in operands to sizeof, _Alignof, or _Generic
RULES

Object designated by a volatile lvalue should not be accessed in the operand of the sizeof operator [CERT_C-EXP44-a-3]
The function call that causes the side effect shall not be the operand of the sizeof operator [CERT_C-EXP44-b-3]
