CERT_C-ERR04 
CERT-ERR04_C (REC) Choose an appropriate termination strategy
RULES

The 'abort()' function from the 'stdlib.h' or 'cstdlib' library shall not be used [CERT_C-ERR04-a-3]
The 'exit()' function from the 'stdlib.h' or 'cstdlib' library shall not be used [CERT_C-ERR04-b-3]
The 'quick_exit()' and '_Exit()' functions from the 'stdlib.h' or 'cstdlib' library shall not be used [CERT_C-ERR04-c-3]
