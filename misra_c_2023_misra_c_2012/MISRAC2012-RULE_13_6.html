MISRAC2012-RULE_13_6 
Rule 13.6 (Required) The operand of the sizeof operator shall not contain any expression which has potential side effects
RULES

The operand of the sizeof operator shall not contain any expression which has side effects [MISRAC2012-RULE_13_6-a-2]
Object designated by a volatile lvalue should not be accessed in the operand of the sizeof operator [MISRAC2012-RULE_13_6-b-2]
The function call shall not be the operand of the sizeof operator [MISRAC2012-RULE_13_6-c-2]
