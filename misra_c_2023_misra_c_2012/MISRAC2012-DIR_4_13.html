MISRAC2012-DIR_4_13 
Dir 4.13 (Advisory) Functions which are designed to provide operations on a resource should be called in an appropriate sequence
RULES

Ensure resources are freed [MISRAC2012-DIR_4_13-a-4]
Do not use resources that have been freed [MISRAC2012-DIR_4_13-b-4]
Do not free resources using invalid pointers [MISRAC2012-DIR_4_13-c-4]
Do not abandon unreleased locks [MISRAC2012-DIR_4_13-d-4]
Avoid double locking [MISRAC2012-DIR_4_13-e-4]
Do not release a lock that has not been acquired [MISRAC2012-DIR_4_13-f-4]
