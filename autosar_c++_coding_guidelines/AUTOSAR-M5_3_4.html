AUTOSAR-M5_3_4 
AUTOSAR M5-3-4 Evaluation of the operand to the sizeof operator shall not contain side effects
RULES

The operand of the sizeof operator shall not contain any expression which has side effects [AUTOSAR-M5_3_4-a-2]
Object designated by a volatile lvalue should not be accessed in the operand of the sizeof operator [AUTOSAR-M5_3_4-b-2]
The function call that causes the side effect shall not be the operand of the sizeof operator [AUTOSAR-M5_3_4-c-2]
