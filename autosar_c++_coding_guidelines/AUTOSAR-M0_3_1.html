AUTOSAR-M0_3_1 
AUTOSAR M0-3-1 Minimization of run-time failures shall be ensured by the use of at least one of: (a) static analysis tools/techniques; (b) dynamic analysis tools/techniques; (c) explicit coding of checks to handle run-time faults
RULES

Do not subtract two pointers that do not address elements of the same array [AUTOSAR-M0_3_1-a-2]
Avoid overflow when writing to a buffer [AUTOSAR-M0_3_1-b-2]
Do not compare two unrelated pointers [AUTOSAR-M0_3_1-c-2]
Avoid accessing arrays out of bounds [AUTOSAR-M0_3_1-d-2]
Avoid division by zero [AUTOSAR-M0_3_1-e-2]
Avoid null pointer dereferencing [AUTOSAR-M0_3_1-f-2]
Avoid overflow due to reading a not zero terminated string [AUTOSAR-M0_3_1-g-2]
Avoid buffer overflow due to defining incorrect format limits [AUTOSAR-M0_3_1-h-2]
Avoid overflow when reading from a buffer [AUTOSAR-M0_3_1-i-2]
Do not check for null after dereferencing [AUTOSAR-M0_3_1-j-2]
