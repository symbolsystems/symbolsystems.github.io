AUTOSAR-M8_5_2 
AUTOSAR M8-5-2 Braces shall be used to indicate and match the structure in the non-zero initialization of arrays and structures
RULES

The initializer for an aggregate or union shall be enclosed in braces [AUTOSAR-M8_5_2-a-2]
Arrays shall not be partially initialized [AUTOSAR-M8_5_2-b-2]
The non-zero initialization of structures requires an explicit initializer for each element [AUTOSAR-M8_5_2-c-2]
