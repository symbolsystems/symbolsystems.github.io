AUTOSAR A7-2-4 In an enumeration, either (1) none, (2) the first or (3) all enumerators shall be initialized
RULES

In an enumerator list, the "=" construct shall not be used to explicitly initialise members other than the first, unless all items are explicitly initialised [AUTOSAR-A7_2_4-a-2]
