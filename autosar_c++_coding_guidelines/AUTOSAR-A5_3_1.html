AUTOSAR A5-3-1 Evaluation of the operand to the typeid operator shall not contain side effects
RULES

The operand of the 'typeid' operator shall not contain any expression that has side effects [AUTOSAR-A5_3_1-a-2]
The operand of the 'typeid' operator shall not contain a function call that causes side effects [AUTOSAR-A5_3_1-b-2]
