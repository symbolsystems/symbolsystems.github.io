AUTOSAR-A15_5_2 
AUTOSAR A15-5-2 Program shall not be abruptly terminated. In particular, an implicit or explicit invocation of std::abort(), std::quick_exit(), std::_Exit(), std::terminate() shall not be done
RULES

The execution of a function registered with 'std::atexit()' or 'std::at_quick_exit()' should not exit via an exception [AUTOSAR-A15_5_2-a-2]
The 'abort()' function from the 'stdlib.h' or 'cstdlib' library shall not be used [AUTOSAR-A15_5_2-b-2]
The 'quick_exit()' and '_Exit()' functions from the 'stdlib.h' or 'cstdlib' library shall not be used [AUTOSAR-A15_5_2-c-2]
