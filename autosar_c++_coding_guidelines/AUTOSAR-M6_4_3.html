AUTOSAR M6-4-3 A switch statement shall be a well-formed switch statement
RULES

An unconditional throw or break statement shall terminate every non-empty switch-clause [AUTOSAR-M6_4_3-a-2]
The final clause of a switch statement shall be the default-clause unless all enumeration values are tested [AUTOSAR-M6_4_3-b-2]
A switch label shall only be used when the most closely-enclosing compound statement is the body of a switch statement [AUTOSAR-M6_4_3-c-2]
Every switch statement shall have at least one case clause [AUTOSAR-M6_4_3-d-2]
A switch expression shall not represent a value that is effectively Boolean [AUTOSAR-M6_4_3-e-2]
