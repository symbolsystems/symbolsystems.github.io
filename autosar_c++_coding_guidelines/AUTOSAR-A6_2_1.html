AUTOSAR-A6_2_1 
AUTOSAR A6-2-1 Move and copy assignment operators shall either move or respectively copy base classes and data members of a class, without any side effects
RULES

Copy assignment operators should not have side effects that could affect copying the object [AUTOSAR-A6_2_1-a-2]
Move assignment operators should not have side effects that could affect moving the object [AUTOSAR-A6_2_1-b-2]
