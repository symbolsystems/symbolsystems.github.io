AUTOSAR A18-5-3 The form of the delete expression shall match the form of the new expression used to allocate the memory
RULES

Never provide brackets ([]) for delete when deallocating non-arrays [AUTOSAR-A18_5_3-a-2]
Always provide empty brackets ([]) for delete when deallocating arrays [AUTOSAR-A18_5_3-b-2]
Properly deallocate dynamically allocated resources [AUTOSAR-A18_5_3-c-2]
